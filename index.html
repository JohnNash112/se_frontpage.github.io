WELCOME TO FEEDBACK SYSTEM

INTRODUCTION

This Feedback system is for Computer Science Department, Tezpur University. This system provide a very efficient and reliable way of collecting Student Feedback for different Course they are enrolled in. Since the last decade, there has been a digital transformation in our society. We are going for digitalisation in working spaces and offices as it ensures proper utilisation of human resource. In our university after each semester students are given feedback forms for each of their respective courses. There are some questions in it for assessing the professor’s teaching methodology and overall view of the student regarding the course. So, we thought of implementing an online based feedback system as it reduces the manual labour in filling those forms. Online based feedback system also makes it easier to store and asses’ data efficiently which is quite cumbersome in the present scenario. Maintaining privacy of students is an important factor as well which is easy to implement in an online based system. The system will generate individual as well as summary of the feedbacks given by the whole batch which then can be assessed by the professors and the HOD for ensuring better learning environment for students.

ANONYMITY

A total Anonymity will be maintained throughout this system revealing nothing to the Admin. The participants can give their feedback without worrying about any consequences, making this system more reliable than other existing systems.
